Your Voice Is Your Weapon

Five tactical voice commands that change the battlefield. No complex key bindings. Just speak and dominate.

SHIELD

Deploy instant cover for your team. Block incoming fire and create tactical advantages.

Cooldown: 15s

LOCAL

Ping enemy locations for your team. Share intel instantly without breaking focus.

Cooldown: 3s

AIRSTRIKE

Mark targets for orbital bombardment. Clear entrenched positions and control zones.

Cooldown: 45s

OVERDRIVE

Temporary speed boost for rapid repositioning. Escape danger or close distance fast.

Cooldown: 30s

SHOCKWAVE

EMP blast that disables enemy voice commands. Force proximity chat only - chaos ensues.

Cooldown: 60s

How SHARD Works

01

Capture Flags

Secure control points across the battlefield. Each flag captured weakens enemy defenses and opens new tactical options.

02

Breach the Base

With flags secured, assault the enemy stronghold. Coordinate voice commands to overwhelm their final defenses.

03

Extract Victory

Eliminate remaining enemies and secure the extraction point. First team to complete the sequence wins.
